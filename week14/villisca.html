THE VILLISCA HOUSE

Location: Villisca, Iowa
Built: Early 1900s

Attic

Experience the thrill of hanging out in the attic, where the mysterious murderer waited patiently for the Moore family to fall asleep. Do bring a blanket-- the ghost children like to play hide-and-seek up there, and that lowers the temperature significantly. They’re cute kids, but carry a rather chilly, drafty air wherever they go.

Starting from $200/night

Bedroom

Remember those nights as a child when you thought the boogeyman (or equivalent) was going to burst out of the closet and snatch you up? Re-live that profoundly unseating sense of dread as you rock back and forth on an old rocking chair in a dead 10-year-old girl’s bedroom. I can’t think of a better way to spend a Saturday evening.

Starting from $450/night

Basement

Unfinished, unfurnished, and undeniably chic, this basement has it all. Weird, inexplicable groans echoing throughout? Check. Random nails* from abandoned DIY projects strewn across the cracked foundation? Check. Boarded up windows? You betcha.
*attach proof of a recent tetanus shot when submitting reservation.

Starting from $200/night
